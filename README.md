#Robot Dog

I made this dog as my passion and my parents never let me keep a pet dog so i made a robot dog for myself which can do a lot of things like: following my voice commands, having eyes ( camera ), an animation on display to make it look like he has life.

i will use it probably as my real life pet on my desk and at my home and i would let him roam here and there so i can have a companion with me at home.

I Made it for my personal use as i was very fascinated by the untitree robot dogs and i wanted to make one ever since that time.
